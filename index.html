Patient Instructions

Important guidelines for your visit and care

Before Your Visit

  • Bring all your current medications and medical records
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment
  • Bring your insurance card and identification
  • Prepare a list of questions you want to discuss

Medication Guidelines

  • Take medications exactly as prescribed
  • Never stop medication without consulting the doctor
  • Report any side effects immediately
  • Keep a medication diary if requested

Lab Tests

  • Fast for 12 hours before certain blood tests
  • Drink plenty of water before urine tests
  • Inform staff of any allergies or medical conditions
  • Follow specific preparation instructions if given

Emergency Contact

  • Call clinic immediately for severe symptoms
  • Use emergency services for life-threatening situations
  • Have your medical information readily available
  • Contact us for appointment changes 24 hours in advance
